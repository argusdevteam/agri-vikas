Agri Vikas 2025

After three consecutive successful Conclaves on Agriculture, Argus News is hosting the next edition in May 2025. The Conclave is a dynamic platform that brings together policymakers, industry leaders, scientists, agripreneurs, and farmers to discuss the evolving landscape of agriculture. With a focus on sustainability, innovation, and policy reforms, the Conclave aims to address key challenges, foster collaboration and knowledge exchange, driving actionable solutions for a more productive, sustainable, and resilient agricultural sector. Whether exploring cutting-edge technologies, policy interventions, or emerging agribusiness trends, Agri Vikas 2025 serves as a catalyst for shaping the future of farming and agribusiness.
