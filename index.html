INDIAN AGRICULTURE - THE ROAD AHEAD

The Indian Agriculture sector is all set to unveil a better tomorrow with the Government striving to boost agricultural production, enhance productivity, and improve farmers' livelihoods by addressing critical challenges and fostering sustainable development through innovative practices and supportive measures. The thrust is to move from conventional methods to modern technology Despite the decline of the share of agriculture in India's GDP to 15 per cent last fiscal year from 35 per cent in 1990-91 it remains the leader in employment. Its share in employment was 44.1 per cent in 2017-18, which has increased to 46.1 per cent in 2023-24.
