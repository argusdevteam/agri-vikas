Odisha: Key Agricultural Trends for 2026

  • Increased, efficient, and technologically advanced farming practices towards record food grain and horticulture production.
  • Modern farm machinery, digital crop surveys for real-time data, and drone technology reducing labour dependency.
  • Stress-tolerant crop varieties and watershed management to manage climate risks.
  • Shift from paddy with emphasis on crop diversification вҖ” oilseeds, pulses, horticulture, floriculture, mushroom farming, and coffee cultivation across six hill districts.
  • Enhanced cold storage infrastructure and export focus to cut post-harvest losses and distress sales.
  • Rapid expansion of allied sectors such as livestock and fisheries.
