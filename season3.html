Harvesting a Smart & Sustainable Agri Eco-System in Odisha

Argus Agri Vikas 2024 - Season 3

Argus Agri Vikas 2024 is a one-day Agriculture Conclave held on 25 May 2024 in Bhubaneswar. The event serves as a platform for collaboration and innovation to harness the transformative potential of agriculture through sustainability. The conclave focuses on emerging challenges and opportunities, sharing best practices, promoting productivity and profitability in Odisha, and strengthening startup collaboration for a smart and sustainable agriculture ecosystem.

    ◆ Most promising practices in Sustainable Agriculture

  • Market linkage, infra and financial support as crucial growth enablers for FPO
  • Social inclusion and the participation of women in FPO
  • Climate change factors affecting FPO success
  • Water management issues related to small farmers and FPOs
  • FPO success stories in Odisha
  • Role of FPOs in achieving SDGs in agriculture
